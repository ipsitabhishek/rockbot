Our warm Condolescences, But we never turn back...

Good Afternoon everyone - Thank you for joining us at the House of the Bourgeoisie this afternoon. I would like to initiate this media briefing by inviting your attention to the matter at hand. Due to a alleged dereliction of security coverage at the residences of the envoys and the emissaries from the nations of the world, we have a wretched moment of bitter sorrow and visceral paranoia at our doorstep. The Ambassador of Malaysia, who was installed by the Government of Russia as a third-party adjudicator - Mr. Dob Dostovesky - was found murdered in the foyer of his apartment. A truly tactless act of malice with the intent to intimidate and disconcert the relations between two neighboring nations. We cannot begin to fathom the amount of pain the government and people of Malaysia must be going through. Neither will we ever be able to really sympathize with the loss faced by the family of Mr. Dostovesky, as well as everyone else with whom he had a congenial bond. He truly was a kindred spirit and always had a solicitous nature when interacting with the people and officials of our own country. However, this is not a time to spend in leisure or endless anguish. This is a time to seek vengeance. Like the first lion deities that roamed the free lands of both our countries - we need to go out and hunt. We need to set pursuit. We need to apprehend. We need to bring to justice without a single kernel of prejudice, those who are responsible for this dastardly act. I promise the people of Singapore, and the people and Government of Malaysia - that we will continue to soldier on with feverish intensity and vitriolic angst till we repay those, who have tried to sow this seed full of malaise among us. Thank you and may god bless you all.
